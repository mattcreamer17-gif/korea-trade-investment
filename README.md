# KOTRA — Prepared Landing

A single-page, bespoke landing brief prepared for leadership at the Korea Trade-Investment Promotion Agency and Invest KOREA. The page is designed to feel native to KOTRA's English and Korean public properties (deep Korean-flag blue, thin red accent, flat institutional rectangles, Noto Sans KR) and to open a thirty-minute conversation with Matt Creamer, Eduba's Chief Revenue Officer, on the layering decisions inside the AI Export Secretary rollout and the Korea Business Center briefing workflow. The page is English by default with an EN / KO toggle in the top bar. Logo is served locally from `logo.png`. All external URLs open in a new tab with `rel="noopener"`. No pricing is surfaced.

Intended URL path: /for/korea-trade-investment-promotion-agency-kotra
